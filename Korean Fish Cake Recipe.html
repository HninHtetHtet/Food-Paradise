Korean Fish Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 450g of cooked fish

  • 2 potatoes,cooked and peeled
  • 2 tablespoons fresh,chopped parsley
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 130g breadcrumbs
  • 1 onion,diced
  • Butter,melted in microwave
