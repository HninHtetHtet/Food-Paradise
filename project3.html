Chinese Pork Dumplings (Jiaozi)



INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce

  • 1 tablespoon seasoned rice vinegar

  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped Chinese chives

  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds

  • 1 teaspoon chile-garlic sauce (such as Sriracha andright)

  • 3 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 egg, beaten

  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped Chinese chives

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce

  • 11/2 tablespoons sesame oil

  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger

  • 50 dumpling wrappers

  • 1 cup vegetable oil for frying

  • 1 quart water, or more as needed



PREPARATION

  1. Combine 1/2 cup soy sauce, rice vinegar, 1 tablespoon chives, sesame seeds,and chile sauce in a small bowl. Set aside.

  2. Mix pork, garlic, egg, 2 tablespoons chives, soy sauce, sesame oil, and ginger in a large until thoroughly combined.

  3. Place a dumpling wrapper on a lightly floured work surface and spoon about 1 tablespoon of the filling in the middle. Wet the edge with a little water and crimp together forming small pleats to seal the dumpling. Repeat with remaining dumpling wrappers and filling.


COOKING METHOD

  1. Heat 1 to 2 tablespoons vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

  2. Place 8 to 10 dumplings in the pan and cook until browned, about 2 minutes per side.

  3. Pour in 1 cup of water, cover and cook until the dumplings are tender and the pork is cooked through, about 5 minutes. Repeat for remaining dumplings.

  4. Serve with soy sauce mixture for dipping.
