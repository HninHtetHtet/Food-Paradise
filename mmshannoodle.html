Shan Noodle

Ingredients

  • 200 gram of chicken (or) pork, chopped
  • 1 pack of dried shan noodle
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 1 ts of ginger, chopped
  • 4 tomatoes, sliced
  • 2 tbs light soy sauce
  • 2 tbs soy bean
  • 1 tbs of sugar
  • 1 tbs of chilli powder
  • 4 tbs of peanut, mashed
  • 3 tbs of vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Boil the dried noodle with water (200 g) until cooked.


  2. Heat oil in a large wok. Fry onion, garlic and ginger until fragrant.


  3. Add chilli powder and stir fried for a half minute.


  4. And add chopped chicken or pork, tomatoes and soy bean, and stir well.


  5. Add 1 cup of water, soy sauce and sugar and cook over a medium heat for 10 minutes until all tomatoes are crushed.


  6. When chicken is cooked, turn off the heat and move aside.


  7. put a handful of noodle into a bowl.


  8. Add 3 tbs of chicken curry, 1 tbs of peanuts and 1/2 table of soysauce.


  9. Add a few spring onions. Serve immediately with vegetable pickles and fried pork rind.
