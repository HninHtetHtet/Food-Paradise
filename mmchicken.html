Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • Chicken (0.5 kg)
  • Oil (0.2 kg)
  • Onion (0.2 kg)
  • Garlic (0.1 kg)
  • Ginger (small amount)
  • Water (1 liter)
  • Curry powder (1 small bag)
  • Salt (small amount)
  • Fish sauce (small amount)
  • Yellow powder (1/2 tea spoon)
  • Chili Powder (3tea spoons)
  • Children powder (2 tea spoons)

Instructions


  1. Peel and wash the onions, garlic and gingers.


  2. Cut the chicken and wash it. The size of the chicken pieces is up to you. Then select with salt and yellow powder.


  3. Pound the onions, garlic,chili and gingers.


  4. Heat oil in a pan on medium heat. You can use any kind of oil.


  5. Add the onions, garlic, chili and gingers and stir them up.


  6. Add the chicken to the pan and put some fish sauce then stir them. Cover a pan and cook for 3-5 minutes.


  7. Add the water. Mix the ingredients together.


  8. Cover the pan again and wait until the meat is cooked. Stir every 3 minutes or so until the chicken is fully cooked.


  9. Add the curry powder, chicken power and salt . Stir again.


  10. When the meat is cooked and everything is combined, turn off the heat and you can prepare to eat.
