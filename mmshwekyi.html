Shwe kyi Senyinmakin

Ingredinets

  • 1/2 cup canola oil,plus more for greasing
  • 2and1/4 cups semolina
  • 2and1/2 cups coconut milk
  • 2and1/2 cups sugar
  • 2 tsp.kosher salt
  • 2 eggs,beaten
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 2 tbsp.white poppy seeds

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 400degree.Grease a 9" wide and 3" deep round cake pan;set aside.Heat semoliba in a 6-qt.saucepanover medium ntil lightly toasted ,10-12 minutes.


  2. Add oil,coconut milk,sugar,salt,eggs,and2 1/2 cups water;cook,stirring constantly,until the mixture comes away from the side of the pan,10-12 minutes.


  3. Add raisins and cook 2 minutes more.Spread evenly into prepared cake pan;sprinkle with poppy seeds and bake 45 minutes.Cool,then slice into squares to serve.
