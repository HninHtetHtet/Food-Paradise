La Phat Thoke

Ingredients

  • 5 teaspoons laphet (fermented tea leaves)
  • 2 teaspoons crispy fried garlic
  • 2 teaspoons crispy fried yellow beans
  • 2 teaspoons roasted peanuts
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame seeds
  • Extra Ingredients (add to your liking)
    • Chopped Tomato
    • Chopped Cabbage
    • Dried Prawns
    • Peeled Garlic
    • Green chili
  • Dressing:
    • 1/2 teaspoon limejuice
    • 3 teaspoons peanut oil

Instructions

  1. First, put laphet (fermented tea leaves) into a bowl. If you want your laphet to be spicy, pound la-phet and green chili together in a mortar.


  2. It'll became a paste. Pour 3 teaspoons of peanut oil. Leave it for awhile so that laphet can soak up the oil.


  3. Put in crispy fried garlic, crispy fried yellow beans, roasted peanuts and sesame seeds in the bowl. Mix them together.


  4. You can also add chopped tomato, chopped cabbage, dried prawns, peeled garlic and green chili as you like.


  5. Put 1/2 teaspoon of limejuice, and some soysauce to your liking.
