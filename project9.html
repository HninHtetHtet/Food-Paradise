Chinese Dumplings (Wonton)



INGREDIENTS

  • 175g minced pork

  • 340 chopped fresh vegetable (Chinese cabbage or celery)

  • 24 (3.5 inch square)wonton wrappers

  • 2 tablespoon Chinese cooking wine

  • 1 teaspoon salt

  • 1/4 teaspoon sugar

  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil

  • 1 teaspoon chopped green onion and ginger

  • moderate soy sauce

  • chicken powder



PREPARATION

  1. In a large bowl, combine pork, sugar, salt, wine, soy sauce, green onion, and ginger.

  2. Blend well, and let stand for 10 minutes.



COOKING METHOD

  1. Place about one teaspoon of the filling at the center of each wonton wrapper.

  2. Moisten all 4 edges of wonton wrapper with water, and then pull the top corner down to the bottom, folding the wrapper over the filling to make a triangle.

  3. Press edges firmly to make a seal.

  4. Bring left and right corners together above the filling.

  5. Overlap the tips of these corners, moisten and press together. Continue until all wrapperrs are used.
  6. Bring some chicken soup to boil.

  7. Carefully place wontons (usually 10-15 wontons for each person) into boiling soup without crowding, and cook for 3-5 minutes till the wontons float to the surface.

  8. It's best to serve with some white pepper powder, several drops of sesame oil, and oyster oil.
